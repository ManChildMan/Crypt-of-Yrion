Yrion is a mystical spirit that kept nature in balance.

Yrion has become currupted due to the work of a Dark Magic Cult.

Your are all that stands between salvation and Damnation.

Destory Yrion and free the world.


Controls:
Movement:
Right Click on a tile to move to it.

Combat:
Left Click to swing weapon.
You will do damage to any enemy in the within range.

Chests/Shops:
Go to the tile imediately infront of it to access.
